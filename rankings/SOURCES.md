# University Rankings 2024 - Data Sources

Here are the sources for the top 4 world university rankings in CSV format.

## 1. Times Higher Education (THE) 2024
✅ **Downloaded**: `THE_2024.csv`
- **Source**: [GitHub (ffddrraaa)](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ffddrraaa/tugas-mk-mpdw/main/Data/Times%20World%20University%20Rankings%202024%20-%20Sheet1.csv)
- **Status**: Available in this directory.

## 2. QS World University Rankings 2024
⚠️ **Manual Download Required** (Login Protected)
- **Kaggle**: [2024 QS World University Rankings 1.1](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/jkanthony/2024-qs-world-university-rankings-11-for-qscom)
- **Opendatabay**: [Global Top 100 Universities 2024](https://opendatabay.com/dataset/global-top-100-universities-2024)

## 3. Academic Ranking of World Universities (ARWU / Shanghai) 2024
⚠️ **Manual Export Required**
- **UniversityRankings.ch**: Go to [ARWU 2024 Results](https://www.universityrankings.ch/results/ARWU/2024) and click "Export CSV" at the bottom of the table.
- **Kaggle**: [Top Universities Ranking 2024](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets) (Search for "Shanghai Ranking 2024")

## 4. U.S. News Best Global Universities 2024
❌ **No Direct CSV**
- **Official Site**: [US News Education](https://www.usnews.com/education/best-global-universities)
- **Note**: US News does not provide public datasets. You may find scraped versions on Kaggle, but they are often incomplete or focused on US National rankings.
